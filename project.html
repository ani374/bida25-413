LUCKY'S BROILER CHICKRN BUSINESS'S Projects

Project 1: BUSINESS Portfolio

Lucky’s Broiler Chicken Business is a locally driven agricultural enterprise focused on supplying fresh, affordable chicken meat and inner parts such as liver, gizzards, and hearts to community members and citizens at large. The business is built on the understanding that access to quality protein is essential for healthy living, and it aims to consistently meet the growing demand within the local market through reliable production and distribution. At its core, the business is committed to delivering freshness and quality. Chickens are raised and processed under controlled conditions to ensure that customers receive clean, safe, and nutritious products. By maintaining high standards in handling and storage, the business strengthens trust with its customers while encouraging healthier food consumption within the community. A key objective of Lucky’s Broiler Chicken Business is to contribute to national food self-sufficiency. By increasing local production and reducing dependence on imported poultry products, the business supports the country’s agricultural development goals and promotes sustainability. This not only strengthens the local economy but also empowers communities through job creation and skills development in agrifarming. In addition to serving the community, the business is designed to be financially sustainable. Through efficient operations, strategic pricing, and consistent supply, it aims to generate profit that supports business growth and improves the standard of living for its owners and workers. Ultimately, Lucky’s Broiler Chicken Business represents a balance between community service, economic empowerment, and long-term sustainability.
