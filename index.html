Welcome to LUCKY'S BROILER CHICKEN BUSINESS short potfolio

Lucky’s Broiler Chicken Business is a growing poultry enterprise dedicated to supplying fresh, high-quality broiler chicken meat and a variety of chicken parts. The business focuses on maintaining strict hygiene standards and ensuring that all products are safe, nutritious, and affordable for customers. It serves both individual households and bulk buyers such as restaurants and events. With a commitment to reliability and customer satisfaction, Lucky’s Broiler Chicken Business aims to become a trusted supplier in the local market while expanding its reach and production capacity..
